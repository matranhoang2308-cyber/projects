   - 65% bounce rate at account step
   - **Decision:** Build guest checkout

   - 68% report mobile difficulty
   - Form complexity is culprit
   - **Decision:** Mobile optimization sprint

3. **Listing Creation Too Complex**
   - 5/6 hosts struggled
   - Too many optional fields
   - **Decision:** Reduce form steps
